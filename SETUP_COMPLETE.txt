TROUBLESHOOTING
---------------
Problem: Console doesn't appear
Solution: Press ~ or  key. Check UE4SS-settings.ini has GuiConsoleVisible = 1

Problem: No data extracted
Solution: Make sure you're in a level (not main menu). Wait for level to fully load.

Problem: Game crashes
Solution: Extract from one area at a time. Restart game between extractions.

Problem: Can't find extracted files  
Solution: Check C:\Users\adkin\source\repos\Psychonauts-2-AP-World---Example\extracted_data\
