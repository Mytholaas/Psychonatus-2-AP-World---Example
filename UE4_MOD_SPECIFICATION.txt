# UE4 Mod Specification: Collective Unconscious Accessibility

## Problem
Collective Unconscious lacks walkable floors connecting all mental world doors.
Without Mental Connection, players cannot reach doors, causing softlocks.

## Solution Required
Add walkable floor paths in Collective Unconscious connecting spawn to all 13 mental world doors.

## Implementation Options

### Option 1: Invisible Floor Meshes (Recommended)
- Spawn invisible static mesh actors with collision
- Connect spawn point to each door
- Add safety net floor below to catch falls
- Preserves natural gameplay feel

### Option 2: Modified Player Movement  
- Enable flight or reduced gravity in CU only
- Easier implementation
- May feel unnatural to players
